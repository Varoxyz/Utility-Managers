Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our Discord bots

How do I add these bots to my server?
For Role Manager and Dust Manager, simply click the "Add to Discord" button on their respective pages. For Security Manager, contact our team through the support server to discuss your server's needs since it's currently available only for large Discord communities.
Are these bots free to use?
Role Manager and Dust Manager are freely available for all servers with optional premium features. Security Manager is currently in development and only available for large communities by request through our support server.
What permissions do the bots need?
Each bot requires specific permissions related to its functionality. During the installation process, you'll see a list of required permissions. We recommend using the default permission sets for optimal performance. The bots will only use permissions that are necessary for their functions.
How do I get support if I have issues?
Join our support server at discord.gg/Wc3tTz5UzW where our team can assist with any questions or issues you might encounter. We provide timely support for all of our bots through dedicated support channels.
What happens to my data when I remove a bot?
When you remove any of our bots from your server, all associated data is automatically and permanently deleted. We do not retain backups or historical data once a bot has been uninstalled, ensuring your server's privacy and data security.
Do these bots work well together?
Yes! Our bots are designed to complement each other. Security Manager handles protection, Role Manager handles role assignments and management, and Dust Manager keeps your server clean. They can be used independently or together for a complete server management solution.
How often are the bots updated?
We regularly update our bots to add new features, fix bugs, and improve performance. Updates are typically deployed without any downtime, ensuring continuous service for your Discord server. Join our support server to stay informed about the latest updates and new features.
